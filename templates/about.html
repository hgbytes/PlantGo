About Our Project

Plant Go is an AI-powered application designed to help farmers and gardeners identify plant diseases quickly and accurately.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make advanced plant disease diagnostics accessible to everyone, from commercial farmers to home gardeners. By leveraging artificial intelligence and machine learning, we aim to:

  • Reduce crop losses due to undiagnosed or misdiagnosed plant diseases
  • Promote sustainable farming and gardening practices
  • Minimize unnecessary pesticide use through precise diagnostics
  • Educate users about plant diseases and their prevention

The Technology

Our Plant Go system is powered by a convolutional neural network (CNN) trained on thousands of images of healthy and diseased plants. The model can recognize:

  • 39 different classes of plant diseases and healthy plants
  • 14 different plant species including common crops and garden plants
  • Various disease symptoms with high accuracy
  • Based on state-of-the-art deep learning architecture

How It Works

Step 1

Take a photo of your plant's affected areas

Step 2

Upload the image to our system for analysis

Step 3

Our AI processes and analyzes the image

Step 4

Get detailed results and treatment options
